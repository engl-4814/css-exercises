The Call of Duty Games

By Kartik Rahar

  • Black Ops One

    This is the first Black Ops game that came out in 2010. It is such a great game with great graphics even today. It has fun modes such as zombies, multiplayer, and campaign. Here are some pictures from the game.

  • Black Ops Two

    This is the second Black Ops game, its just as good as the first one, with more maps and fun things to do. Its also super realistic in visuals and physics

  • Black Ops Three

    This is the thrid black ops game and its great but in my opinion its worse than the first 2. I say that because it starts to get unrealistic by giving players jetpacks that allow them to fly up in the air.

  • Black Ops Four

    This is the fourth black ops game and the latest one. Its a great game but in my opinion the zombies mode isnt as good as the older games. The multiplayer is great though.
