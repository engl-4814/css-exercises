Preparing for your trip

To prepare for your trip, you will want to do some basic research on where you are going and what you will need

I recommend researching the following:

  • What airline goes there
  • which hotel you will stay in
  • what the transportation is like there. Will you need to rent a car?
  • which type of clothing should you bring?
