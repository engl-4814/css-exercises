Welcome to the Capital of Sierra Leone

We are very glad that you decided the visit the continent of Africa. On this site, you will explore the beautiful country of Freetown and visit the country's best housing locations and landmarks to visit during your stay. But first, here is a little mroe information about the country. Freetown is a port city and the capital of Sierra Leone, in West Africa. It’s known for its beaches and historical role in the transatlantic slave trade. The old town’s centuries-old Cotton Tree is a symbol of emancipation. On the waterfront is the King’s Yard Gate, through which former slaves walked to freedom.
