The Executor is easily the best one in the game. The flagship of the legendary Sith Lord, Darth Vader. The executor is a terrifying display of power that will help you strike fear into the hearts of those who would dare oppose the mighty Galactic Empire.

The Death Star is the Capital Ship that you turn to when the executor fails. If the Executor strikes fear into their hearts, then the death star makes them need a change of underwear. Capable fo destroying entire planets, it is the obvious choice for when the less aggressive methods fail. The only downside is no one will be left on that planet to get your point, it only serves to instruct the rest of the galaxy.
